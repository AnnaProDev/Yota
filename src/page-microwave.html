Common microwave
Problems We Fix

There are a lot of things that can go wrong with your microwave oven. Our technicians are highly trained and experienced in all aspects of microwave appliance repair. We are reliable, so you can count on us for any microwave repairs or maintenance. So, here are some of the most common problems we fix:
Door won't close or open by Itself
There are a few things that can cause it, including a faulty door switch, door latch, or hinges.
Display is blank or doesn't show anything
If your microwave ovens display is blank or doesn't show anything, it could be caused by a bad circuit board. At times the buttons may also not be responsible if the membrane switch or the control board is defective. Call our microwave repair professionals to get your appliance checked.
Oven won't start Up or turn on
Usually, this is brought up by a defective door switch or circuit board. It can also be that the thermal fuse or ceramic fuse needs replacement.
No heat coming from the oven
If your microwave oven isn't heating up, it could be caused by a bad magnetron or voltage diode. It is very important to feel safe at home, so don't hesitate to contact our microwave repair specialists.
Microwave bulb doesn't light
Faulty bulb socket and bad wiring are mainly the cause of bulbs, not lighting.
Microwave doesn't shut off
Possible causes may be due to a faulty door interlock switch or a bad smartboard.
Microwave runs and then stops
This can be caused by a faulty door switch, defective touchpad, transformer, or fan motor.
Microwave Making Noise
Possible causes include a faulty plate or drive motor, bad Magnetron, or high voltage diode. It can also be due to a defective cooling fan. Make sure you contact our microwave repair technician to examine the issue.
